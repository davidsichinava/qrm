Quantitative Research Methods

What this course is about?

We are going to learn the basics of statistical analysis. Apart from pure theory, this course also aims at teaching the fundamentals of data analysis with SPSS, a popular (and propiertary, sorry) statistical data analysis package.

What will I need for this course?

  • Going through the required readings (they are written in human language, which is quite rare in the nerdy world of stats folks;
  • Doing lab assignments during our class meetings AND at home;
  • Submitting those assignments ON TIME
  • Attending class meetings. Obviously, this is strongly recommended, although please let me know beforehand if you are going to skip the class. Note that despite skipping the class you will also have to turn over the lab assignments.

Where are the course materials?

We will have two required readings:

  • Agresti and Finlay’s Statistical Methods for Social Sciences and

  • Field’s Discovering Statistics Using SPSS
